About performance

This play has been on the stage for 400 years! The indomitable Katarina is a true woman: she is beautiful, smart, proud and wayward. It is impossible to curb such a nature - only to conquer! And only a real man can do this. How will the duel of two outstanding, strong personalities end? Irreconcilable enmity or passionate, burning love?
A seething, incendiary musical performance with songs, dances, bright acting work is one of the best creations of the creative tandem of Valentina and Alexei Erenkov!
