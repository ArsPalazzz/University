About performance

According to the memoirs of contemporaries, this story “touched everyone so much that for the whole winter they only talked about it, and instead of“ How is your health? they asked: “Have you read the Kreutzer Sonata?” The story, not yet printed, was rewritten by hand, the emperor did not give permission for publication for a long time, the empress was shocked, Tolstoy's wife felt insulted, and readers either enthusiastically welcomed the bold work or fiercely criticized it. Still would! Not a single author had dared to speak so frankly about family and marriage until that moment.
“Husband and wife made a commitment to live together for the rest of their lives. But suddenly they realized that they hate each other, they want to disperse and still live - then this is that terrible hell from which they drink, shoot, kill or poison each other ...
I am Pozdnyshev. The one who killed his wife ... What metals are in the sun and stars - you can find out, but what exposes our disgustingness is difficult, terribly difficult ... There is one thing in me - I know that not everyone will soon find out.
History of one mistake? One crime? Or maybe the single most fearless act?
The performance is a confession, the performance is a mirror, the performance is a reflection.
