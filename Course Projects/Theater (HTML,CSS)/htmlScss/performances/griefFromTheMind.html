About performance

Another classic that is always up to date!
“Bah, all familiar faces!” Dates, disappointments, gentlemen, servants, hints, intrigues and, finally, a ball!
Bright, amazingly funny images are combined into an incredible kaleidoscope, and magnificent, imaginative music sets a frantic pace for the whole action.
Interesting? Yes! Funny? Very!
But the story of how stupid townsfolk destroy a sincere, intelligent and honest young man ...
A classic play by Alexander Griboyedov in a new interpretation by Sergei Kovalchik.
