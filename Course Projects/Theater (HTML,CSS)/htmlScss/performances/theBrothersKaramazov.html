About performance

The Brothers Karamazov is the last book by F. M. Dostoevsky. Despite the fact that Dostoevsky was already a very famous and revered author, this work literally shocked Russian society. The Brothers Karamazov is a detective story, a love story, and a philosophical treatise. It incredibly intertwines fatal passion, cold hatred, vile intent, philosophical reasoning, criminal silence, moral quest, cruelty and sacrifice. Imaginary virtue turns into indifference, lust turns into true love, and unlimited freedom turns into spiritual chaos. The story of bright personalities, men and women, longing for love, truth and justice.
