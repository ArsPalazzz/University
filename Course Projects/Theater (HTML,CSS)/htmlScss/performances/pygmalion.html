About performance

A performance about real and imaginary aristocrats, good manners, sincere feelings and, of course, about love!
On the streets of London, three characters accidentally meet - Colonel Pickering, professor of phonetics Higgins and flower girl Eliza. The men bet that the professor can teach an uneducated commoner how to talk like an aristocrat. A joke experiment leads to very serious consequences ...
What separates "dirt" from "prince"? Does the manner of speech influence the way of thinking? How can you see yourself with different eyes? What is success in life?
Subtle humor, classic plot, wonderful music and brilliant acting in the great comedy by Bernard Shaw "Pygmalion"!
