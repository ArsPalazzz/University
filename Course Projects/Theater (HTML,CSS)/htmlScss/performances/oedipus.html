About performance

Oedipus solved the riddle of the Sphinx, became the ruler of Thebes and married the widow of the former king. The city prospered...until the plague came. King Oedipus is in despair: he cannot understand the reason for the wrath of the gods. The Delphic oracle reveals the secret - in Thebes there is a man who killed the former ruler of Thebes, King Laius. And until the killer is found and punished, pestilence and famine will not stop.
Oedipus is determined to find the criminal and save his people. But he does not even suspect what a terrible discovery awaits him ...
