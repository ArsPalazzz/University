About performance

Six friends get together - six young, beautiful, unmarried women! They have not seen each other for a long time, and there is a reason: one of them is pregnant and is going to give birth. Initially, the party was planned as a nice ladies' gathering: light alcohol, light snacks, as well as memories, congratulations, gossip and reproaches - a little bit of everything. But something went wrong...
Touchingly gullible, shockingly frank, defiantly sexy, funny, boring, strong, weak - women will appear on stage in all their guises. And, of course, there are men in the play - after all, all women's problems ultimately come down to them!
