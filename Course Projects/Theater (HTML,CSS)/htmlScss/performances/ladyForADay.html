About performance

The lyrical comedy "Lady for a Day" directed by Dmitry Astrakhan is a story about a poor woman Annie and her friends helping the unfortunate woman to see her only daughter Louise and arrange her marriage to the heir of a Spanish count. Once Annie was happy, loved and rich. But evil fate separated her from her daughter and left her without a penny in her pocket. Having gone bankrupt, she is forced to sell apples in one of the quarters of New York.
This simple, cheerful performance will make your hearts shrink more than once, because it tells about the most important thing - about the victory of good over all the vicissitudes of fate, about true friendship and about the great power of love.
