Kobeni Higashiyama

Kobeni Higashiyama (東山 コベニ Higashiyama Kobeni?) was a Public Safety Devil Hunter who started as a new recruit working under Makima's experimental squad.
Kobeni is a petite young woman with short black hair tied in a ponytail. On her face are four moles, two under her left eye, one in the lower right side of her mouth and one on her left cheek. She normally wears a professional suit and she has two hair-clips.
At the newbie welcome party, she wore a hand-me-down dress from her older sister which was considered to be cute by a couple other devil hunters.
Kobeni is exceptionally cowardly with few moments of courage. Her first confrontation with the Eternity Devil lead to her being paralyzed with fright and babbling. She is shown to easily crack under pressure and breaks down in tears when the group becomes trapped in the hotel and she is confronted with the possibility of them all starving to death without any rescue.
