Power

Power (パワー Pawā?) is a Blood Fiend (血ちの魔ま人じん Chi no majin?) and a Public Safety Devil Hunter who is part of Makima's special squad.
Power has the appearance of a young woman with strawberry blonde hair that reaches down to her mid-back. As a fiend, she has light red horns protruding from the top of her head, red and yellow eyes that form a cross pattern, and sharp teeth with defined fangs.
Power usually wears the standard dress shirt, tie, and slacks required of Public Safety Demon Hunters. Normally, she wears breast pads to increase the size of her bust. She almost always wears her jacket hanging halfway off her body giving her an untidy, half-dressed appearance.
During Operation: Super-Smart, Power donned a pair of glasses and tied her hair back in a ponytail.
If Power accumulates too much blood her horns became larger and more curved, and she can sprout additional pairs of horns from her head.
